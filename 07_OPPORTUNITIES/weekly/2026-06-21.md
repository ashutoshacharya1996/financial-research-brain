- **Supply chain delegation confirmed:** BEL placing ₹52.82cr electro-optics subcontract to PARAS via official regulatory filing — Tier-1 PSU to Tier-2 private company supply chain architecture is on record, not anecdotal; per Exchange Filing — PARAS — 2026-06-02

**Risks:**
- **HAL delivery track record:** LCA Mark-1A already slipped once (FY26 → Aug/Sep 2026); if slips again, HAL's 10–12% FY27 revenue guidance is directly at risk

- **10-way competition on MALE UAV:** Probability of any single company winning is uncertain; split order reduces winner-takes-all but also means no company gets the full ₹30,000cr
- **Budget dependency:** All award timelines assume sustained Union Budget defence allocation; any compression in FY28 defence budget (presented February 2027) would defer awards
