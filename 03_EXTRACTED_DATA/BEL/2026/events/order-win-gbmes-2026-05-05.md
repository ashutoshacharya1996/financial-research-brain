## Named Catalysts

- **GBMES Contract — ₹1,251 crore**: Signed with Ministry of Defence for Ground Based Mobile ELINT System to Indian Army
- Product designed by DLRL (DRDO's Defence Research & Development Laboratory) Hyderabad; manufactured by BEL — fully indigenous
- Capability: Networked intelligence system detecting/classifying/locating all radar types; intercepts and analyzes communication signals
- Strategic significance: ELINT (Electronic Intelligence) systems are critical for air defence; enhances India's electronic warfare capability
- Totally indigenous — qualifies under Defence Acquisition Procedure's Indigenously Designed Developed and Manufactured (IDDM) category

## Named Risks

- Single customer (MoD/Indian Army) for strategic platform — delivery timelines dependent on government programme schedules
- ELINT systems are classified — limited public disclosure on specifications or margin profile
