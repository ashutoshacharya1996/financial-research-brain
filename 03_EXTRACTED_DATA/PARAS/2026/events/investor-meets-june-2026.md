## Management Commentary — Demand

- Paras Defence is described as "India's only listed company in the electro-optic and imaging segment for defence" — supplying night-vision systems, space cameras, and electromagnetic pulse (EMP) protection equipment
- Positioned as a niche supplier across: anti-drone systems, defence optics, space imaging
- Sight-25HD optical system: market opportunity stated as >5,000 units across armoured vehicles, air defence systems, FRCV (Future Ready Combat Vehicle), FICV (Future Infantry Combat Vehicle), and RCWS (Remote Controlled Weapon Station) platforms over 3–7 years
