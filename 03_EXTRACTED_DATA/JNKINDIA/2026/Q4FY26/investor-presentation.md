## Management Commentary — Capacity / Expansion

"In its initial year of operations, JNK Chemdist Technologies contributed ~7% share to the Group revenue." — Mr. Arvind Kamath

JV structure: JNK India 51%, Chemdist 49%. Focus: green hydrogen, ethanol-to-chemicals, industrial water solutions (advanced membrane technology)

Fabrication facility: Mundra, Gujarat — 5,000 MT installed capacity; ~20,243 sq. metres; multi-product SEZ; proximity to deep draft port; 50 kWp solar power installed; ASME R, U, S certified; ISO 9001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, ISO 14001:2015
