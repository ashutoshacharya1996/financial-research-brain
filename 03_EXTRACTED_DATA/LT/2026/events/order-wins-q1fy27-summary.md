## Named Catalysts

- **JSW Steel Mega order** (₹10,000–15,000 Cr): Engineering, procurement and installation of Blast Furnaces and Steel Melt Shops at Ballari (Karnataka) and Paradip (Odisha) — part of JSW Steel's capacity expansion from 35 MTPA to 50+ MTPA by 2031.
- **BCGCL Large order** (₹2,500–5,000 Cr): Coal-to-Ammonium-Nitrate, Odisha — LSTK Package-4, Nitric Acid and Ammonium Nitrate Plant, 2,000 TPD capacity.
- **BCGCL Significant order** (₹1,000–2,500 Cr): Ammonia Synthesis Unit (LSTK Package-3), Coal-to-Chemicals project, Odisha.
- **B&F Large order** (₹2,500–5,000 Cr): Three real estate projects — 10-tower residential Hyderabad (each 180m, B2+55F); luxury tower Worli Mumbai (260m, B5+63F); villas/hotel in Karnataka.
- **PT&D Significant — Middle East** (₹1,000–2,500 Cr): EPC for one 380 kV + two 132 kV extra-high voltage substations for prestigious Middle East clients.
- **GeoStructure Significant** (₹1,000–2,500 Cr): Largest-ever piling order from JSW Utkal Steel at Paradeep Integrated Steel Plant (30 lakh running metres); two IWAI Ship Repair Facilities (Patna + Varanasi, NW-1); India's first yacht marina at Mumbai Harbour (Mumbai Port Authority).
- **B&F Significant** (₹1,000–2,500 Cr): Oberoi Realty — 7 high-rise residential towers Gurugram; proving ground for international company in Haryana.
- **L&T–Exail MCM Partnership** (May 14, 2026): L&T as prime contractor for Unmanned Mine Counter-Measure Suite for Indian Navy's 12 MCMV programme; Exail (France) as technology partner. No financial quantum disclosed.
