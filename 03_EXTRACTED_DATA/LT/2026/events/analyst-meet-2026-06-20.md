## Guidance

- FY27 guidance reiteration: 10–12% growth in both order inflows and revenue
- "H1 will be softer due to execution headwinds, expect an anticipated pickup in H2 with PPM margins broadly stable"
- H2 FY27 pickup predicated on Middle East conditions normalizing
- Margins: "PPM (Project and Product segment Margins) broadly stable" — no compression guidance

## Management Commentary — Demand

- Middle East supply chain disruptions cited as the reason FY26 revenue grew 12% (below 15% guidance)
- FY27: management expects Middle East to normalize in H2 — enabling H2 FY27 revenue acceleration
- "H2 to be stronger as Middle East conditions normalize"

## Management Commentary — Margins

- PPM margins guided as "broadly stable" for FY27
- No quantitative margin guidance beyond stability signal

## Management Commentary — Capacity / Expansion

- Strategic plan "Lakshya 2031" (five-year plan outlined in Q4 FY26 call) — details not updated at this meet
- Defence portfolio expanding: K9 Vajra-T programme active; MALE UAV bid submitted; multiple MoD programmes

## Named Catalysts

- **K9 Vajra-T Contract (~$900M / ₹7,500 crore)**: Indian Army contract for self-propelled howitzers; in order book; multi-year execution underway at Hazira, Gujarat
- **MALE UAV Tender (₹30,000 crore)**: LT submitted bid before June 16 deadline; split award (L1 + L2) means LT could win partial share even as second bidder; evaluation ongoing
- **Order book ₹7.4 trillion (+28% YoY)**: Record level provides multi-year revenue visibility; 10–12% FY27 revenue guidance well-covered
- **H2 FY27 recovery**: Middle East normalisation is the swing factor for FY27 earnings beat

## Named Risks

- FY26 revenue below guidance (12% actual vs 15% guided) — Middle East execution risk not fully resolved
- H1 FY27 explicitly flagged as soft — near-term earnings pressure
- MALE UAV: 10 bidders, split order structure — outcome uncertain; LT could win nothing
- Lakshya 2031 plan requires sustained double-digit growth across multiple business segments
