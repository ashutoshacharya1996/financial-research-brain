## Management Commentary — Corporate Action

From regulatory filing (May 22, 2026):
- "This is with reference to, and in furtherance of, our disclosures dated May 11, 2026, and May 19, 2026, in connection with the Composite Scheme of Arrangement ('Scheme') amongst Anchemco India Private Limited (formerly known as Andasia Private Limited) ('Transferor Company'), Asia Investments Private Limited ('Transferee Company' for Part C and 'Demerged Company' for Part D of the Scheme) and Gabriel India Limited ('Resulting Company' or the 'Company')."
- "The Scheme has become operative effective from May 22, 2026 ('Effective Date')."
- "With effect from the Effective Date, the Transferor Company stands amalgamated with the Transferee Company and dissolved without being wound up (Part C of the Scheme) and the Demerged Undertaking of the Demerged Company stands transferred to and vested in the Company on a going concern basis (Part D of the Scheme)."
