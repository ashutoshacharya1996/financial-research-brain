## Game Theory View

**The prize:** Indian industrial companies breaking into Middle East, Africa, and SE Asia as China+1 manufacturing gives them cost and geography advantages.

**Who captures it:** Companies with proven international execution AND proprietary product technology (JNKINDIA's niche fired heater equipment). Generic EPC contractors face more competition.

**The risk:** Middle East is the primary international market — LT's execution problem highlights that this region has unique supply chain challenges. A concentration risk, not just an opportunity.

## Counter Arguments
