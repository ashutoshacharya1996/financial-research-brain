## Key Signal Changes This Run

### Upgrades

1. **India defence production ₹1.78 lakh crore FY26 (+15.6%) — GOVERNMENT CONFIRMATION**
   - Macro-level corroboration of the entire defence theme thesis. Not company-level guidance — actual production data from Ministry of Defence. This is the baseline against which all defence orders and order books should be assessed. Private sector at 24% / ₹42,000 crore all-time high confirms structural shift, not cyclical uptick.

2. **PARAS first-ever management guidance: 30-40% revenue growth FY27 + FY28**
   - Single highest-quality signal upgrade this run. A company with no earnings call, no investor presentations previously disclosed, now issuing two-year revenue guidance at three separate institutional investor forums (including a London roadshow with GIC, Temasek, global funds). Qualitative shift in management communication posture.

3. **BEL pipeline decomposed at investor meet: QRSAM + P-75I + AMCA + data centres**
   - FY27 >₹55,000 crore order inflow target now has four named sources for the first time. Reduces single-programme concentration risk in the thesis (was QRSAM-only previously). P-75I and AMCA are multi-year programmes; even early-stage orders would be multi-crore.

4. **GABRIEL sunroof JV margin 15.1% FY26 — above 12-14% management guidance**
   - Highest-quality EV theme signal this run. Confirms the sunroof JV is operationally profitable above guidance in Year 1. EV two-wheelers +30% is the first concrete number attached to Gabriel's EV exposure narrative.

5. **GABRIEL ₹260-270 crore total capex confirmed — including new fastener vertical**
   - Fastener plant is a net-new product vertical. ₹60-70 crore committed. Signals management's capital allocation confidence is extending beyond existing growth segments.

### Downgrades / Cautions

1. **LT H1 FY27 "softer" explicitly flagged**
   - First time management has given an explicitly bearish near-term quarter characterisation. Prior tone was "Middle East is execution, not demand." Current tone introduces a temporal dimension: execution headwinds in H1, recovery only in H2. Watch Q1 FY27 results as a potential earnings miss signal.

2. **PARAS guidance is unconfirmed by order book**
   - 30-40% guidance implies ₹621-669 crore FY27 revenue. Current order book ~₹928 crore covers ~1.4-1.5x the implied FY27 target — barely adequate. For guidance to hold, PARAS needs fresh order wins in FY27. Sight-25HD >5,000 unit opportunity is an addressable market, not a confirmed pipeline.

## Notes for Screener

- **Defence theme at 9.0**: The macro government confirmation (₹1.78L cr production record) is the most important single signal this run. It validates that the procurement environment driving HAL/BEL/PARAS order books is operating at record levels — not just company-specific fortune. This should push existing defence opportunities to "Strong Buy" conviction.

- **PARAS guidance is a step-function change**: No company without an earnings call issues two-year revenue guidance at a London roadshow with GIC, Temasek, and global funds unless management is highly confident. This is the first time PARAS has crossed from "interesting small-cap defence story" to "management-guided growth thesis with institutional backing."

- **GABRIEL crosses from thesis to evidence**: Prior runs had GABRIEL as an EV pivot *intent* story. This run delivers *operational numbers*: ₹434 crore sunroof revenue, 15.1% margin above guidance, 170,000 units, Mahindra and Mobis new customers. The story is no longer speculative.

- **QRSAM binary catalyst**: BEL's QRSAM (₹30,000 crore; BEL share ~₹21,000 crore) is expected to sign in July 2026 — 8 days away from this run date. If it signs, BEL order book lifts ~40% overnight. If it slips again (third time), confidence in BEL's FY27 order inflow guidance will need to be reduced. Watch for NSE exchange filing from BEL.

- **LT is a deteriorating position**: H1 FY27 soft + margin compression + Middle East execution risk + PAT -3% Q4. LT belongs in the portfolio as a long-term Lakshya 2031 theme, but near-term earning risk is real and now explicitly management-acknowledged.
