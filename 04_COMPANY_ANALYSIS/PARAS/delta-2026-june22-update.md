## New Mentions This Period

- **FY27 + FY28 revenue guidance: 30–40% growth per year** — First-ever management guidance from a company with no earnings call. Source: investor meet commentary (multiple institutional investor interactions, June 2026). Unprecedented change in management communication posture.

- **Sight-25HD optical system pipeline — >5,000 units over 3–7 years** — Market opportunity across FRCV, FICV, RCWS, armoured vehicles, and air defence systems. Not a confirmed order; framed as addressable market. Establishes that Paras's electro-optics segment has a long runway beyond BEL subcontracts.

- **India's only listed electro-optic and imaging defence company** — Unique market positioning articulated explicitly by management in investor meets. Differentiates PARAS from broader defence PSUs and private players.

- **London roadshow (June 15-16)** — Kotak Institutional Equities organized. Management meeting global fund managers including those at InterContinental London Park Lane. Signals intent to attract foreign institutional investment at a scale not previously pursued.

- **India defence production FY26: ₹1.78 lakh crore (+15.6% YoY)** — Macro validation; private sector share at 24% all-time high (₹42,000 crore). PARAS is precisely in this category. Sector-level growth creates platform for PARAS's individual order wins.

## Key Signals Summary

1. **First-ever revenue guidance: 30–40% growth FY27 and FY28** — New Mention — Strength: High
   Evidence: "Management of Paras Defence is guiding for 30-40% revenue growth in FY27 and FY28 while maintaining or growing profitability." Issued during institutional investor meetings (June 2026). Paras Defence has never previously provided revenue guidance in accessible sources.

2. **Three institutional investor engagements in June 2026** — Inflection Up — Strength: Medium
   Evidence: June 9 (Citigroup — GIC, Temasek, Birla MF, Tata AIA); June 15-16 (Kotak — London global funds); June 25 (PL Capital — Armoured Alpha conference). Volume and quality of meetings signals management's confidence in the story and active effort to build institutional ownership.

3. **India defence production ₹1.78 lakh crore — private sector at 24% all-time high** — New Mention — Strength: High (macro)
   Evidence: Government data released June 17, 2026. Private sector produced ~₹42,000 crore — all-time high share. PARAS is a private sector defence manufacturer; this confirms the structural shift in India's defence ecosystem toward private Tier-2 suppliers like PARAS.

4. **Sight-25HD pipeline: >5,000 units over 3–7 years across FRCV/FICV/RCWS** — New Mention — Strength: Medium
   Evidence: Market opportunity articulated at investor meets. Not a confirmed order. Depends on government programme timelines for FRCV and FICV (both unconfirmed programmes). But scale of opportunity — if even 20% converts — would add ₹500+ crore to order book over the period.
