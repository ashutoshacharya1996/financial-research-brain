## New Mentions This Period

- **India defence production FY26: ₹1.78 lakh crore (+15.6% YoY)** — HAL's own FY26 revenue (₹33,088 crore) represents ~18.6% of total India defence production — making HAL the dominant single contributor. Record production number is not separate from HAL; HAL IS a material portion of that number.

- **Defence exports ₹38,424 crore (record)** — HAL's LCA Tejas Mk-1A export interest (Argentina, Malaysia, Egypt cited in prior periods) is embedded in the context of India becoming a defence exporter. Record export number is a macro tailwind for HAL's export aspirations. Note: HAL's current exports are not a material proportion of this ₹38,424 crore — but the directional signal confirms India's credibility as a defence supplier is rising, which benefits LCA marketing.

- **Private sector 24% share (all-time high)** — Not directly positive for HAL as PSU, but indicates a healthy defence manufacturing ecosystem. HAL benefits from private sector partners (supply chain) being viable and growing.
