## New Mentions This Period

- **Sunroof JV EBITDA margin 15.1% (FY26) — above management's own 12–14% target** — Highest-quality signal in this update. Year-one at scale, margin already above guidance. Implies operating leverage ahead: as volumes grow from 170,000 → 250,000+ units, fixed cost absorption improves further.

- **EV two-wheelers +30% YoY** — First concrete number on EV exposure. Gabriel supplies shock absorbers and suspension to EV two-wheeler OEMs; +30% growth is above total auto industry growth rate. Confirms EV segment is growing faster than Gabriel's overall +19% Q4 revenue.

- **Mahindra and Mobis named as new OEM customers** — Mahindra is India's fastest-growing auto OEM (SUV segment) and largest EV player. Mobis is Hyundai's Tier-1 global supplier. Both are premium/EV-oriented OEMs. Gabriel adding these two signals product quality validation at the highest end of the auto customer spectrum.

- **Genos plant commercial production by Q4 CY26** — New suspension manufacturing facility; production starting October–December 2026. Revenue contribution from FY27. Not speculative — construction underway.

- **SK operations started** — Another new manufacturing site; already in operations. Adds to Gabriel's distributed manufacturing footprint.

- **Fastener plant: ₹60–70 crore capex committed** — New product vertical entirely. Gabriel moving into fasteners (presumably auto-grade precision fasteners). No revenue yet; timeline not stated.

- **Lubricants JV** — New business area confirmed in investor presentation. No financial details.

## Key Signals Summary

1. **Sunroof JV margin 15.1% FY26 — above 12–14% management guidance** — New Mention — Strength: High
   Evidence: "FY 2026 revenue stood at INR 434 crores and EBITDA of INR 65.4 crores with a margin at 15.1%." Management's own target was 12–14%; the JV is already above guidance in its first year at meaningful scale. Operating leverage should improve further as volumes scale past 170,000 units.

2. **EV two-wheelers +30% YoY — concrete evidence of EV exposure materialising** — New Mention — Strength: High
   Evidence: "30% jump in EV two-wheelers." Gabriel's suspension and shock absorber products serving the fastest-growing auto sub-segment. First time a number has been attached to the EV growth narrative.

3. **Mahindra and Mobis as new OEM customers** — New Mention — Strength: High
   Evidence: "secured new orders from OEMs like Mahindra and Mobis." Both are premium and EV-oriented OEMs — validates Gabriel's product quality for higher-margin vehicle segments.

4. **Genos plant online Q4 CY26 + SK already operational** — New Mention — Strength: Medium
   Evidence: "SK operations have started, and Genos construction is ongoing, with commercial production expected by Q4 of the calendar year." Capacity additions underway; FY27 will be first full year with expanded manufacturing footprint.

5. **Total capex plan ₹260–270 crore confirmed for first time** — New Mention — Strength: Medium
   Evidence: ₹150 crore (suspension) + ₹50 crore (sunroofs) + ₹60–70 crore (fasteners). Management is deploying capital at scale — not a growth story on paper.
