## The Big Macro Development You Need to Know

**India defence production: ₹1.78 lakh crore in FY26. All-time record. Announced June 18.**

Here's why this matters more than a press release:

When you buy BEL, HAL, or Paras, you're implicitly betting on India's defence spending growing. That bet now has official government confirmation. In five years, defence production went from ₹84,643 crore to ₹1.78 lakh crore — more than doubled. In the last year alone, it grew 15.6%.

Defence exports grew even faster: ₹38,424 crore in FY26, up 62.7% from the prior year. India now sells defence equipment to 80+ countries — up from basically zero a decade ago.

The private sector's share hit an all-time high of 24%. That's the part relevant to Paras (private sector supplier) and Gabriel India — it means the government is deliberately moving some of its spending away from PSUs toward private companies.

The Nifty India Defence index (a stock market index tracking defence companies) hit a new all-time high on the day of this announcement (June 18). Paras Defence was the standout — it rose 18.1% in a single day.
