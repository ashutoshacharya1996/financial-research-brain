## Blocker Assessment

**Strongest blocker:** Motilal Oswal's ₹1,266 target implies 35× FY28 EPS — premium multiple with ZERO margin of safety. For the thesis to hold at current valuation, PAT CAGR of 55% FY26-FY28 requires ALL of the following:
- Genos plant (auto glass) commercial production Q4 CY26 — on schedule
- Mahindra and Mobis ramp to significant volumes within 12–18 months
- Sunroof JV volume scale-up to more than ~170,000 units (FY26 level)
- Fastener plant ramp-up (₹60-70 crore capex committed, production TBD)
- Core suspension business maintaining/expanding margins while carrying ₹260-270 crore capex load

Any one delay cascades: if Genos slips 1-2 quarters, FY28 PAT shortfall vs 35× PE compresses the thesis.

**Secondary blockers:**
- Scheme of Arrangement specifics: The nature of the "demerged undertaking" from Asia Investments Pvt Ltd is not fully disclosed in the filing. This was confirmed as POSITIVE (NCLT-sanctioned, going concern basis) but the precise business that now vests in Gabriel India is unknown. Need to read the earlier May 11, 2026 disclosure for full details.
- 35× PE is Motilal Oswal's target — other analysts may price differently; the thesis depends heavily on the JV ramp being confirmed in FY27 results
